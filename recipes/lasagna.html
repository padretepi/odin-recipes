Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, garlic over medium heat until well browned
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes and water. Season, simmer, covered, for about one and a half hours, stirring occasionally
  4. Boil salted water, cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta chese with egg, parsley and salt
  6. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C
  7. Assemble
  8. Repeat layers and top with mozarella and parmesan. Cover with foil
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an addition 25 minutes
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving
